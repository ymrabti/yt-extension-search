YouTube Search – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

📌 Overview

YouTube Search ("the Extension") is a browser extension that allows users to quickly search YouTube from the toolbar, right-click context menu, or by selecting text on any webpage. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

In short: We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information. The Extension operates entirely on your device.

🔒 Data Collection

The Extension does not collect any data of any kind. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, etc.)
  • No browsing history or activity is tracked
  • No search queries are stored or logged
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used
  • No analytics or telemetry data is gathered

⚙️ Permissions Used

The Extension requires the following permissions solely for its core functionality:

  • Tabs – Used to detect if the current tab is blank so search results can open in the same tab instead of creating a new one.
  • Context Menus – Used to add a "YouTube IT" option to the right-click menu for quick searching of selected text.
  • Content Scripts (all URLs) – Used to display a floating YouTube search icon near selected text on any webpage. No page content is read, modified, or transmitted.

🌐 Third-Party Services

The Extension does not communicate with any third-party servers, APIs, or services. The only external interaction is navigating to youtube.com when the user initiates a search.

💾 Data Storage

The Extension does not store any data locally or remotely. No user preferences, search history, or cached information is saved.

👶 Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected from any user, it complies with COPPA and similar regulations.

📝 Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Extension after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
